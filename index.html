Three Eyed Monster on grass plain

This monster is walking a cross a field to reach the near by village. It uses it 4 legs to run quickly across the grass. It uses its 3 eyes to shoot lazers at near by homes or people. This monster can also possess peoples minds and control them making them do what it wants. When people are possessed their eyes turn bright red and they become very pale.It uses its possessed army of people to attack other villages. This monster is more active during the day and spends most its nights hiding/sleeping away from any people.
